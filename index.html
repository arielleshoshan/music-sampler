About

Tame Impala was created by artist, Kevin Parker. Kevin Parker is an australian musician best known for his psychedelic music project, Tame Impala. Parker writes, records, and performs all of Tame Impala's music. There are currently four released albums: Innerspeaker (2010), Lonerism (2012), Currents (2015), and The Slow Rush (2020).

Parker has recieved numerous awards for Tame Impala's music and has recieved four Grammy nominations. The music can be described in several genres, from alternative/indie to pop-synth and psychedlic pop/rock. The music is a melody of incredible sounds that produce goosebump sensations.

Tame Impala's music reminds me of looking through a kaleidescope and seeing the multitude of patterns and colors. There are so many sounds overlaid that bring so much depth to the music as a whole. When I listen to music I listen for the sound and not the lyrics. Tame Impala has been a long time favorite and always will be.
